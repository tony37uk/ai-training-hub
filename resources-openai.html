OpenAI Resources

Official learning resources for ChatGPT, the OpenAI API, GPT models, and building with the company behind the generative AI revolution.

OpenAI is the research company behind ChatGPT, GPT-4, DALL·E and the Whisper speech model. Their official docs, cookbooks, and academy are an excellent starting point whether you're a casual user, a prompt engineer, or a developer building production applications.

💡 Where to start: New users should explore ChatGPT and the OpenAI Academy. Developers should dive into the Platform docs and the OpenAI Cookbook for practical code examples.
