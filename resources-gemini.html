Google Gemini Resources

Official learning resources for Gemini — Google's multimodal AI — covering the Gemini app, AI Studio, the Gemini API, and building with Vertex AI.

Gemini is Google's flagship family of multimodal AI models. It's available as a consumer chat assistant, inside Google Workspace (Docs, Gmail, Sheets), and as a powerful API for developers via AI Studio and Vertex AI.

💡 Where to start: Try the Gemini app first to see what the models can do. Developers should head to AI Studio for fast prototyping before moving to Vertex AI for production.
